About this game

Ludo Sing™ is a classic board game played between friends and family. Play the dice game of kings! Recall your childhood!

Ludo Sing is a cross-platform multiplayer game that supports Desktop, Android, iOS, and Windows mobile platforms at the same time. This game also supports offline mode, where the player can play with Computer or, Local multiplayer (pass and play mode).

Ludo Sing is also a favorite game of Bollywood superstars and Indian cricket stars. Play this dice game, Ludo Sing. Best casual game in board games.

Ludo Sing follows the traditional rules and the old school look of the Ludo game. The Ludo game has evolved throughout the centuries to come to your mobile phone. Just like the kings and queens of India’s golden age, your fate depends on the roll of the Ludo’s dice, and your strategy of moving the tokens effectively.
